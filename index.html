Edgar the Explorer

Open-Source iOS platform game

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

We dreamed of a FOSS (Free/Libre and Open Source) platformer on iOS. We didn't find any, so we ended up making it. Feedback, reuse and contributions are welcome!

dgar, an old mustached activist (you), has to inspect an underground laboratory. He fights against experimentation on alien beings at Greenalien.

  • Free movement: left, right, high jump, long jump
  • Use minecarts and elevators
  • Avoid contamination by escaped aliens
  • Rush or explore slowly: it's up to you!
  • The stopwatch makes the score
  • FOSS (free and open-source): reuse or contribute
